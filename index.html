GreyToast
v3.0 · Windows Toast Notification Generator
GreyToast is a browser-based toolkit for generating Windows 10/11 Toast Notification PowerShell scripts. It is designed to support red team operations, security awareness training, and social engineering assessments in authorized environments.
Intended Use
Authorized penetration testing, red team engagements, phishing simulations, and internal security awareness programs where explicit written permission has been obtained.
Legal Disclaimer
This tool is provided for educational and authorized security testing purposes only. Unauthorized use against systems or individuals without explicit consent may violate computer fraud laws, Computer Misuse Act, and equivalent legislation in your jurisdiction. The author assumes no liability for misuse.
What It Does
Generates PowerShell scripts that trigger Windows Toast Notifications spoofing trusted system apps (Teams, Defender, Edge, etc.) to simulate phishing, vishing, credential harvesting, and malware delivery scenarios during authorized red team operations.
